Specializations

  • Multiplier Effect: Its offensive cyberwarfare is used as a multiplier along with its military operations. Where the military is at, their cyberwarfare team will be actively attacking in the same area.

  • Focuses on: Intelligence gathering, neutralization of adversarial capabilities, and deception of adversaries.

EncroChat attacks

  • Emma 95 targeted EncroChat, an encrypted phone network used by criminals. It attacked EncroChats server and pushed a malware update to over 60,000 devices. The malware bypassed the encryption allowing France to retrieve all the data in transit.

Detection plan

  • Threat intelligence sharing with allies.
  • France aligns its cyber operations with its military operations, so if Frances military is operating somewhere, then their cyber team will be as well.
  • Integration of intrusion detection systems to detect their use of malware.
  • Integration of Endpoint Detection and Response for advanced threat detection.
  • Security Information and Event Management to identify patterns and anomalies within logs.
