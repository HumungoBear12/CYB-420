NATO-EU Doctrine

NATO Cyberwarfare Laws & Principles

Cyber Defense as Collective Defense

NATO treats cyberattacks with the same seriousness as conventional attacks under its collective defense policy.

Cyberspace as an Operational Domain

In 2016, NATO officially recognized cyberspace as a domain of warfare, like air, land, and sea.



EU & NATO Country Cyber Laws

Cybersecurity Act (EU)

Strengthens ENISA and creates an EU-wide cybersecurity certification system.

Germany's IT Security Act

Requires essential services to implement security measures and report cyber incidents.
