Bureau 121

North Korea's Elite Cyber Warfare Division

Bueau 121 is the Democratic People's Republic of Korea's cyber warfare unit, tasked with conducting both defensive and offensive cyber operations. Established under the Reconnaissance General Bureau, Bureau 121 stands as the shield of our sovereignty and the sword that strikes at the heart of our adversaries' cyber vulnerabilities. Our mission is to defend our nation against external cyber attacks while gathering intelligence on our adversaries and protecting the Kim family dynasty.

Together, under the guidance of our supreme leader, we will continue to break new ground and ensure the prosperity of our great nation.

Signed,

Jo Kyong Chol

Head of Security Command
