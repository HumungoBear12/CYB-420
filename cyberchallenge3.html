Defending Against CyberWarfare Attacks On The Weapons Program

Identifying Cyber Challenge

Many other Nation-States are very scared of how many weapons North Korea has. We are always ready to attack anyone and everyone so other countries make it a goal to take out our weaponry. We need to stay ahead of their cyberwarfare attacks on our weapons program by implementing sufficient controls to our systems.

In a Cyberwarfare attack against our weapons program, other nation-states would hope to gain access to inforamtion on where we hold weaponry. They also would want to gain access to systems that have the ability to detonate bombs or have access to new weapon designs. We need to ensure full protection of our weapons by being very dilligent with our controls and procedures to stop attacks that could disrupt our mission capabilities.

Plan to Address the Cyber Challenge

Implementation Plan Prior to an Attack

Configurations and Procedures

  • Complete an in-depth risk assessment on all weapon systems to find vulnerabilities
  • Mitigate all known vulnerabilites no matter the severity score. Implement all security controls to make it as tight as possible while ensuring usability.
  • Implementation of zero trust principles on all weapon system architecture.
  • Implement advanced threat detection tools such as Vulnerability scanners, EDR, SIEM, and SOAR
  • Create an isolated network for all weapon systems
  • Pay informers of other nation-state cyber teams to feed us information about potential attacks against us.

During an attack against our weaponry

Incident Response

  • After initial threat detection, initiate IRP team for investigation to identify entrance location, attack vector, and affected systems.
  • Isolate all affected systems and shut down all weapon system to ensure the attackers cannot detonate anything.
  • Based on attack type implement containment measures.
  • Activley monitor all system including initial unaffected systems to minimize the intrusion attempt.
  • Have you offensive focused personnel try to track who is attacking.

After Action Plan for Countering PSYOPs

Post Attack

  • Conduct thorough analysis of the attack and its vectors and methods to enter the system.
  • Based off the thorough investigation, implement new security controls to all of the weapon systems and network to fix system vulnerabilites.
  • Review and enhance entire weapon systems architecture.
  • Based off the information that was gained, start actions of moving critical weaponry to new locations.

Retaliation Attack Setup

  • Trace attack back to the source to identify the nation-state sponsored attack. Based on the country, identify that countires most important infrastructure. Devise a plan against that infrastructure and attack it. This is another level of defense to show that whoever performs cyber attacks against North Korea will soon after have an attack attempt against them.
