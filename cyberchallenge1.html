Cyber Challenges

Critical Infrastructure Cyberattacks

Identification

Cyberattacks targeting North Korean critical infrastructure. Examples include but not limited to power grids, transportation systems, and healthcare facilities. Attacks on systems and facilities like these can have severe consequences like economic disruption and loss of life.

Incident Prevention and Response Plan

Implementation Plan (Pre-Attack)

Cybersecurity Standards

  • Implement stringent cybersecurity standards for critical infrastructure operators.
  • Require regular security assessments and penetration testing.
  • Require incident response training for employees.

Incident Response Planning

  • Develop comprehensive incident response plans for critical infrastructure sectors.
  • Conduct regular drills to test response capabilities.

Cooperation with Allies

  • Collaborate with international partners to share information and coordinate responses to cyberattacks.

Action Plan (During Cyberattack)

Rapid Response

  • Quickly identify and contain the attack.
  • Activate the incident response team to coordinate the response effort.

Mitigation and Recovery

  • Implement measures to mitigate the impact of the attack, such as network segmentation and system isolation.
  • Develop a recovery plan to restore critical services.

Law Enforcement and Intelligence Cooperation

  • Work with law enforcement and intelligence agencies to identify and apprehend the attackers.

Post-Action Plan

Incident Reflect & Improvement

  • Conduct a post-incident review to identify lessons learned.
  • Update security policies and procedures based on the findings of the review.

Enhancing Cybersecurity Measures

  • Implement additional security measures to prevent future attacks.

Cooperation with Allies

  • Continue to collaborate with nation allies to share information and coordinate responses to cyberattacks.
