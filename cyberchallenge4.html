Cyber Challenges

Weaponization of Misinformation and Digital Exploits

Identifying Cyber Challenge

The use of PSYOPs (psychological operations) in the cyberspace is a growing challenge in the current age of cyberwarfare. The purpose of these operations is to manipulate public opinion and trust between people and communities to cause a national disturbance.

Cyber PSYOPs utilize weapons to exploit social media, create fake news, and create digital propoganda to disrupt communities, influence elections, and manipulate public opinion. For example, the recent growth of AI and deepfake technology, according to Fortinet, have been used in social engineering scams where fake audio and videos have been used to spread misinformation using automated bots, create conspiracy theories about current issues, and conduct identity theft and financial fraud (Fortinet 2023).

Plan to Address the Cyber Challenge

Implementation Plan Prior to an Attack

Develop PSYOP Recognition

  • Develop a misinformation detection tool that is trained to monitor online content to flag potential PSYOP activities.
  • Deploy bot detection systems that monitor network traffic analytics to identify any coordinated bot attacks to a service to minimize mass sharing of false information.
  • Promote programs that educate others on how to recognize misinformation such as AI and deepfake technology to minimize the spread of fake information.

Detecting Ongoing Cyber PSYOPs

Collaboration

  • Work with and utilize social media platforms to help flag and request removal of harmful content being posted. Identifying any threats, suspicious accounts, hashtags, or activity.

Use AI to Combat AI

  • Utilize AI to update and monitor detection algorithms based on the its observation of the current PSYOP attack.
  • Use AI to detect spikes in user activity from newly created accounts or a deviation in user behavior to flag for further investigation.

Conduct a Offensive Operation

  • Conduct an offensive operation on the PSYOP's infrastructure that is deploying bots. According to the Journal of Medical Internet Research, bots are automated online accounts that "use behaviors like excessive posting, early and frequent retweeting of emerging news, and tagging or mentioning influential figures" (Himelein-Wachowiak et al, 2021).
  • Launch an offensive attack on botnets such as DDoS to disrupt their infrastructure, honeypots to mislead and gather intelligence on the botnet, or sinkholing to redirect the attack to a different environment.

After Action Plan for Countering PSYOPs

Update Cybersecurity Systems

  • Perform an investigation on how the misinformation got through our infrastructure, identifying what systems were affected, how network traffic was affected, and what malware was used during the operation.
  • Use the investigation to update and enhance the systems security infrastructure and fix system vulnerabilities. As well as integrate new detection algorithms and update the current algorithms based on the insights gained from the attack.

Continue Collaborations

  • Continue to work with more online platforms to improve the nations defense against misinformation campaigns, allowing more information and insight on how PSYOPs affect other online systems. According to WEF, collaboration is "vital to effectively address the multifaceted challenges posed by AI-enabled misinformation and disinformation" (Li et al., 2024).
