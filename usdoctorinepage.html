US Federal Laws

Computer Fraud and Abuse Act

Criminalizes unauthorized access to computers, targeting hacking, data theft, and cyber espionage, with penalties for cybercrimes.

Cybersecurity Information Sharing Act

Promotes sharing of cyber threat data between companies and federal agencies, with liability protections.



US State-Level Laws

California Consumer Privacy Act

Grants California residents rights over their personal data, including the right to know, delete, and opt-out of data sales.

New York SHIELD Act

Requires businesses to implement security measures for New York residents' data, expanding breach notification requirements.
